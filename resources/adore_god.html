To help calm the busy thoughts that might come between you and a simple awareness of God's presence, focus your mind on something concrete and particular that is connected, in some way, to God. It may be a term of endearment for God, or some work of his hands like a scene of natural beaty, or the flame of a candle. As your thoughts begin to wander, which they will, renew your intention to make yourself present to God by gently returning your attention to this focal point.

Adapted from "Mystically Wired", by Ken Wilson © 2010
