Lord Jesus Christ, Son of the Living God, have mercy on me, a sinner.
You may shorten it to suit, e.g., "Lord Jesus Christ, have mercy on me."

Connect the prayer to your breathing: "Lord Jesus Christ" [during inhale], "have mercy on me" [during exhale]. Breathe from the abdomen. If connecting the prayer to your breathing is confusing, don't bother. Try doing the prayer for a specified period, e.g. five or ten minutes. As your thoughts wander, gently return your focus to the words of the prayer.
