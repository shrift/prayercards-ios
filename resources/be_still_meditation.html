"Be still and know that I am God." Psalm 46:10

Meditate on this verse as though memorizing it, starting with the first word and adding a new word each time: "Be...", "Be still..." then "Be still and..." then "Be still and know...", until you have completed the verse. Take your time. Use a full relaxing breath in and out for each segment. Place special emphasis on the newly introduced word (indicated by italics) as you say it aloud or silently.
