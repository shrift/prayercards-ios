1. At the end of the day, quiet yourself and invite the Spirit to remind you of blessed moments throughout the day.

  2. Review the day from the point of waking up in the morning until bedtime, paying attention to blessed moments or events for which you can be thankful. Nothing is too small, ordinary, or mundane to be thankful for.

  3. Close with The Lord's Prayer or a similar favorite prayer.
